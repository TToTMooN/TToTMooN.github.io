About me

I am a PhD student in robotics at University of California, Berkeley adviced by Prof. Masayoshi Tomizuka. I received B.S. in Mechanical Engineering from University of Michigan, Ann Arbor, and B.S. in Electrical and Computer Engineering from Joint Institute, Shanghai Jiao Tong University.

My research interest lies in Robotics (skill learning, control and optimization) and Autonomous driving (prediction, behavior generation).
